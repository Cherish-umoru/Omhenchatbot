About Us
Unu Omhen, meaning "Good Mouth" in the Edo language, is Lagos' most prestigious fine dining Nigerian restaurant. Located in the heart of Ikeja GRA, we offer an exclusive and unforgettable dining experience celebrating the rich and diverse flavors of Nigerian cuisine with a premium touch.
At Unu Omhen we believe that food is not just nourishment — it is culture, heritage and an experience to be savored. Rooted in Edo tradition and inspired by the finest culinary standards, every dish at Unu Omhen tells a story of authenticity, passion and excellence.
We do not just serve food. We create memories.

Q: Do you cater for private events and celebrations? A: Absolutely! We love celebrating special moments with our guests. Please chat with Omhen or contact us via our website for special event and private dining enquiries.
Q: Is traditional Nigerian attire acceptable? A: Without a doubt! We proudly celebrate Nigerian culture and traditional formal wear such as Agbada, Guinea and Aso-oke are not only acceptable but warmly encouraged. Wear your culture with pride!
Q: What makes Unu Omhen unique? A: We are Lagos' premier fine dining Nigerian restaurant rooted in Edo heritage and culinary tradition. Every dish is freshly prepared with premium ingredients, every table receives personal attention and every visit is designed to be an unforgettable experience. As we say — Good Mouth, Great Food!
Q: How do I give feedback or make a complaint? A: We take every piece of feedback seriously. Simply chat with Omhen our AI concierge who will professionally handle your feedback and ensure it reaches the right people immediately.
